Vegetarian and Vegan Online Cooking Courses

Are you looking to learn more about meatless or plant based meals? Here are some resources to check out for plant based cooking:

  • Veecoco

    • German twins Lukas and Samuel Schanderl went vegan to improve their health and want to share their passion for vegan food while helping others understand how delicious it can be. Veecoco is designed for cooking enthusiasts who are looking to expand their knowledge of plant-based recipes.

    Veecoco offers 18 different online vegan cooking courses covering pastry, brunch, Thai, Italian, Vietnamese, German, cheese, sourdough and more! Plus, they offer Q&A sessions with chefs, downloadable materials, shopping lists, and a membership community.

  • Plant Based Meal Plan

    • If you are looking to meal plan with plant based foods, the meal plans from Plant Based Meal Plan have what you need. Toni Okamoto is a cookbook author and Michelle Cehn is a YouTube personality and together they have designed meal plans that make it more accessible for those who want to eat plant-based but need help getting started.

    They offer a variety of bundled meal plans whether you want one or more weeks of recipe ideas. Another bonus? The cost per meal for these recipes comes out to just $1.20.

  • Global Vegetarian

    • Parkash Bains, the founder of Global Vegetarian, grew up eating mostly vegetarian recipes at home, but also some meat based dishes. After seeing the condition of how chickens lived as an adult, she decided to go fully vegetarian.
