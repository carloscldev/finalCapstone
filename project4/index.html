Welcome to Cooking as a Hobby!

Whether you're a seasoned pro or just starting out in the kitchen, we hope you'll find something here that inspires you to get cooking. On our website, you'll find a collection of our favorite recipes, as well as some helpful tips and resources for home cooks.

In the Recipes section, you'll find a wide variety of dishes to try, from appetizers and entrees to desserts and drinks. We've included something for everyone, so you're sure to find something that suits your taste.

In the Tips and Resources section, you'll find helpful information on topics like knife skills, mise en place, and ingredient substitutions. We've also included a list of our favorite kitchen tools and equipment, as well as some recommendations for cookbooks and food blogs.

We hope you enjoy your time here and find something that inspires you to get cooking. Don't be afraid to try new things and have fun in the kitchen - that's what it's all about!

Happy cooking!
